The Standard
We Hold Ourselves To.

Three principles that shape everything we create — from a single set to a full bridal look.
01

Premium Materials

We source only salon-grade gels, high-end embellishments, and rigorously vetted tools. Quality is non-negotiable — because what touches your hands should be nothing short of exceptional.

02

Handcrafted Precision

No mass production. No shortcuts. Every nail is individually designed, hand-painted, and inspected. The care invested in each piece is what makes the difference between good and unforgettable.

03

Cruelty-Free Always

Every product we use is strictly vegan and cruelty-free. Beauty should never come at another's expense — this is a commitment we made from day one and will never compromise on.
