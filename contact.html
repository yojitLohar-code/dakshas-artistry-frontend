FAQs

Quick Answers

How long do custom press-on nails take to ship?
Custom sets are handcrafted with care — please allow 5–7 business days for production, followed by shipping. We'll keep you updated at every step.
Do you offer bridal packages?
Yes! We offer complete bridal packages that include both nails and makeup. Enquire via the form and we'll put together a personalised quote for your date.
Can I visit the studio without an appointment?
Our studio works exclusively by appointment to give every client dedicated time and attention. Please book via WhatsApp or the contact form above.
What nail sizes do you cater to?
Every custom set includes a sizing kit. We cater to XS through XL and can accommodate extra-wide or petite nail beds — no standard sizing here.
Are your products cruelty-free?
Absolutely. All products used at Daksha's Artistry are 100% cruelty-free, vegan-friendly, and free from harsh chemicals like formaldehyde and toluene.
Do you ship outside India?
We currently ship across India. International shipping for select countries is coming soon — drop us an email and we'll notify you as soon as it's live.
