Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

At Daksha's Artistry, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or make a purchase.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the website, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and services, or when you contact us. This includes:

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, phone number, and shipping/billing address.
  • Account Data: Encrypted passwords and One-Time Passwords (OTPs) used for account verification.
  • Payment Data: We do not store your credit card or UPI details on our servers. All payment data is handled securely by our third-party payment gateway partners (e.g., Razorpay/Stripe) using 256-bit encryption.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect or receive to:

  • Fulfill and manage your orders, payments, and digital access.
  • Send you order confirmations, shipping updates, and administrative information.
  • Protect our website against fraud and unauthorized transactions.

3. Sharing Your Information

We only share information with third parties that perform services for us or on our behalf, including payment processing, data analysis, email delivery, and hosting services. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to access or store information regarding your session (such as keeping items in your shopping cart or keeping you logged in).
